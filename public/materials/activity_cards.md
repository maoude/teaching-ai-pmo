# Activity Cards

## Card 1: Scope Master

Mission: Define the project foundation.

Deliver:

- Five stakeholders
- Three measurable success criteria
- One-sentence project vision

## Card 2: WBS Architect

Mission: Break the Smart Campus App into deliverables.

Deliver:

- Level 1 project name
- Level 2 major deliverables
- Level 3 sub-deliverables

Rule: Do not submit only a task list.

## Card 3: Schedule Optimizer

Mission: Reduce the first 15-week schedule into a realistic 12-week plan.

Allowed actions:

- Parallel work
- Scope prioritization
- Temporary support
- Testing automation

Forbidden shortcut:

- Removing all testing

## Card 4: Dashboard Detective

Mission: Diagnose project health from week 6 data.

Evidence:

- Progress: planned 50 percent, actual 38 percent
- Cost: planned 20,000 USD, actual 24,000 USD
- Critical bugs: 12
- Completed modules: planned 4, actual 2

Deliver:

- Diagnosis
- Main cause
- Corrective action

## Card 5: Crisis Controller

Mission: Prepare a PMO recovery briefing.

Structure:

1. What happened?
2. What is the impact?
3. What corrective action is proposed?
4. What sponsor decision is needed?
