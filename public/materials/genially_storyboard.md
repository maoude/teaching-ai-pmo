# Genially Storyboard

## Title

Smart Campus App PMO Challenge

## Screen 1: Mission Briefing

Text: The university president wants a Smart Campus Mobile App launched before the next semester. You have 12 weeks, 40,000 USD, and a small technical team.

Button: Start PMO Mission

## Screen 2: First Decision

Question: What should the PM do first?

Choices:

- Start coding immediately
- Define scope and stakeholders
- Buy software tools
- Ask for more budget

Correct feedback: Good decision. Scope and stakeholders create the foundation for the project.

Wrong feedback: This creates risk. Without scope and stakeholders, the team may work on the wrong priorities.

## Screen 3: Stakeholder Quest

Task: Identify five stakeholders and three measurable success criteria.

Submit instruction: Write your answers on the worksheet, then continue.

Badge: Scope Master

## Screen 4: WBS Builder

Task: Build a deliverable-oriented WBS with three levels.

Hint: Use deliverables, not only tasks.

Badge: WBS Architect

## Screen 5: Schedule Rescue

Scenario: The first plan takes 15 weeks. The sponsor requires 12 weeks.

Question: Which action is most professional?

Choices:

- Hide the delay
- Remove testing
- Add features
- Parallelize work and prioritize scope

Correct feedback: Good. This is realistic schedule compression.

Badge: Schedule Optimizer

## Screen 6: Dashboard Detective

Dashboard:

- Planned progress: 50 percent
- Actual progress: 38 percent
- Planned cost: 20,000 USD
- Actual cost: 24,000 USD
- Critical bugs: 12

Question: Is the project healthy?

Correct feedback: No. It is late, over budget, and technically unstable.

Badge: Dashboard Detective

## Screen 7: Week 8 Disaster

Scenario:

- Database integration fails.
- QA reports many bugs.
- Sponsor refuses deadline extension.
- Team is overloaded.

Question: What should the PMO do?

Correct choice: Freeze scope and focus on critical deliverables.

Wrong choices:

- Add features
- Stop testing
- Hide the delay

Badge: Risk Analyst

## Screen 8: PMO Briefing

Task: Prepare a three-minute briefing.

Required structure:

1. What happened?
2. What is the impact?
3. What corrective action is proposed?
4. What decision is needed from the sponsor?

Final badge: Crisis Controller
