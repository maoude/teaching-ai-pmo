Section 3 — Project Description · وصف المشروع
Project Summary (50–70 words) ملخص التصميم
Students act as a PMO team hired to rescue the Smart Campus Mobile App — a 12-week university project under budget and schedule pressure. Through five gamified quests, they define scope, build a WBS, optimize a schedule, interpret earned value indicators, and present a professional recovery briefing, supported throughout by AI tools for scaffolding, visualization, and formative feedback.

Pedagogical Problem (50–70 words) المشكلات التعليمية
Project management students often master theoretical concepts in isolation but struggle to integrate them under realistic pressure. They rarely practice interpreting earned value data, diagnosing delays, or communicating corrective decisions professionally. This module addresses that gap by placing students inside a live simulated project crisis, compelling them to apply planning, monitoring, and communication skills together — as a real PMO team would.
