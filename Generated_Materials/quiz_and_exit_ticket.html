Quiz and Exit Ticket

Formative Quiz

1. What should a project manager do first?

A. Start implementation B. Define scope and stakeholders C. Buy software D. Assign random tasks

Correct: B

2. What is a WBS?

A. Employee list B. Deliverable-oriented decomposition C. Budget form D. Meeting agenda

Correct: B

3. A 15-week plan must become 12 weeks. What is most professional?

A. Remove testing B. Hide delay C. Re-plan using parallel work, scope prioritization, and resources D. Add features

Correct: C

4. Planned progress is 50%, actual progress is 38%. What does this suggest?

A. Ahead B. Late C. Complete D. Automatically under budget

Correct: B

5. Actual cost is higher than planned cost. What should the PM investigate?

A. Cost overrun causes B. Dashboard color C. Logo D. How to avoid reporting

Correct: A

6. SPI below 1 usually means what?

A. Ahead B. Behind schedule C. No cost issue D. Closed

Correct: B

7. CPI below 1 usually means what?

A. Cost performance is inefficient B. Under budget C. Schedule complete D. No scope risk

Correct: A

8. During crisis, which action is most responsible?

A. Prioritize critical deliverables B. Add non-essential features C. Stop communication D. Ignore bugs

Correct: A

9. What is project control?

A. Reading the title B. Comparing actual performance to plan and taking corrective action C. Choosing fonts D. Naming the team

Correct: B

10. What should a PMO briefing include?

A. Problem, impact, corrective action, sponsor decision B. Excuses C. Only screenshots D. Complaints

Correct: A

Exit Ticket

  1. What is one difference between monitoring and controlling?
  2. What was the most important corrective action in your group plan?
