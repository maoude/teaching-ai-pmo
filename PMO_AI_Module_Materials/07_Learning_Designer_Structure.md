# Learning Designer Structure

Use this structure to enter the module into the Learning Designer tool.

## Module Metadata

Title: From Plan to Control: Managing a Project Like a Real PMO

Topic: Project Management, WBS, scheduling, monitoring, control, risk, corrective action

Level: Licence / Master

Duration: 180 minutes

License: CC-BY-NC recommended

## Teaching and Learning Activities

| Time | Activity | Learning Type | Teacher Role | Student Role | Tool |
| ---: | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 15 min | Opening scenario and first decision poll | Acquisition + Discussion | Present scenario and launch poll | Vote and justify first PM action | Genially + Mentimeter/Wooclap |
| 30 min | Planning and project charter | Acquisition + Inquiry | Explain planning concepts | Identify stakeholders and success criteria | Slides + NotebookLM summary |
| 35 min | WBS workshop | Production + Collaboration | Guide groups and check WBS quality | Build WBS for Smart Campus App | Worksheet + Canva/Napkin/Mermaid |
| 35 min | Schedule optimization | Practice + Collaboration | Introduce 15-week constraint | Create 12-week recovery schedule | Google Sheets |
| 35 min | Dashboard and KPI analysis | Practice + Inquiry | Explain tracking indicators | Diagnose project health | Google Sheets + Quizizz |
| 20 min | Crisis simulation | Discussion + Production | Run scenario and facilitate choices | Prepare PMO recovery briefing | Genially |
| 10 min | Final briefing and reflection | Production + Reflection | Evaluate and summarize | Present and complete exit ticket | Briefing template + Google Forms |

## AI Integration

AI tools are used for:

1. Generating and refining realistic scenarios.
2. Producing WBS examples and checking completeness.
3. Creating quiz questions and formative feedback.
4. Generating visual diagrams and slide drafts.
5. Supporting student reflection and instructor preparation.

## Gamification Integration

Gamification mechanics:

- Five levels
- Quests
- Points
- Badges
- Branching decisions
- Immediate feedback
- Final PMO achievement category
